Stun Bats

Deep in the gloomy [Redacted] Forest, the staff of a secret cybernetics research facility are presented with an emergency: a glitch in the power maintenance system caused a brownout, unlocking the cage in which dozens of cybernetic bats, known as Bat Bots, were confined.

As the head security officer, it is your job to prevent them from escaping. Armed with your trusty stun gun, disable as many Bat Bots as you can. Once you're done, you can leave the rest of the cleanup to the facility's special recovery forces.

Play with Your Friends, Kids, or Grandkids

Bat Bots are pesky automatons. Luckily, you brought your fellow security officers to help! Compete in the Competitive Mode to see who can stun the most Bat Bots.

Visit the Past

Bat Bots is Team Throwback's love letter to an age of games past. Our intention is to give folks a modern twist on classic gameplay, a window into their past hours spent at the arcade, while also being enjoyable for kids.
